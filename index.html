Welcome

In spite of the increasingly green world that we live in, cars are still the most popular form of transportation in the US. Given that, it's no surprise that car accidents are one of the leading causes of death. Still, there are plenty of us who have learned to drive safely without ever bothering to learn much about what goes on under the hood. We wonder questions like:

  • Why do I have to change my oil?
  • Why do I have to wait for the car to warm up in the winter before the heat comes on?
